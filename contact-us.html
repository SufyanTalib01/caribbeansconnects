FAQ

Click on the register link at the bottom of the home page or https://caribbeansconnects.com/register/

Once logged in, go to the Manage Profile tab > Set up profile > Fill out all mandatory fields > submit and wait for confirmation. https://caribbeansconnects.com/account/add-profile/

You can add multiple profiles, go to Manage Profile > Set up Profile > Fill out mandatory fields > Submit and this will create another profile. https://caribbeansconnects.com/account/add-profile/

Go to Find Job > Click on a job > Fill out all relevant fields > Copy and paste your profile URL located at the bottom of your talent profile page > Click on Apply Now.

Go to Manage Profile > Message and Forum Settings. https://caribbeansconnects.com/account/message-forum-settings/
